Our Services

Coyesa Company Limited is a private company that was established in Tanzania as a Civil Works and Building contractor as well as Consultant in Engineering related field and Land Management and Development.The following are the services that we provide:

Water Resources Infrastructures.

The water engineering services offered by Coyesa Company Limited for both public and private sector clients are; Feasibility studies of Water Supply and Sanitation, Detailed Design of Water Supply Systems, Sanitation Systems and Treatment Plants, Detailed Design and Construction Supervision of Hydraulic Structures / River Engineering, Water Resources Management Studies, Borehole Drilling and Dam Constructions, Wastewater Management.

Borehole Drilling and Dam Constructions.

Services offered by the company include Geophysical & Hydrogeological Survey,Borehole Drilling, Pump Testing, Water Quality Analysis, Designing of Water Supply Project, Pipe Works & Fittings,Construction of Water Raiser Tanks,Construction of Domestic Points, Pumps Installation, Construction of Barbed Wire Chain links, Construction of Pump House, Construction of concrete OGB plinth,Construction of concrete brickwork cabinets.

Geotechnical Investigation (Soil Testing).

Services offered in the field:Hydrological Survey,Borehole Drilling of Six (6) Pits Drilling, Standard Penetration Test (SPT), Dynamic Cone Penetration tests,Soil Profiling & Sample Collection.

Road Works Construction Services.

Our structural expertise spans a wide variety of Constructions industries like other Contractors specifically in Building and Civil Works in Highway and Transportation Engineering Division and Structural Engineering forms, Water Supply Engineering as well as Geotechnical and Materials Testing Engineering. Coyesa Company Limited offers Construction services for civil, commercial, industrial, and residential structures such as Bridges, Buildings and Rehabilitation of structures. In architectural division we are dealing with Interior and Landscape designing, Development Consultancy and Administration of Construction services

Telecom Infrastructures Services.

Services offered:Construction of Palisade Fence include Stone Pitching, Supply Imported G7 Backfill Material, Geofabric membrane Type PT15, Gate Ramp construction, and Installation of palisade fencing system

Petrol Station & Warehouse Construction Services.

Services that we offer:Site Clearance & Foundation Works,Concrete Works, Welding Works, Roofing Works, Electrical Installation, Fire System Installation, Finishing Works, and Landscaping.

Building Construction Services and Creativity

Services that we offer:Designing and Drawing, Site Clearance & Foundation Works,Concrete Works, Masonry Works, Roofing Works, Electrical & AC Installation, Fire System Installation, Finishing Works and Landscaping.

Environmental Consultancy Services

These includes; Environmental Planning and Social Impact Assessment, Environmental Auditing, Environmental Monitoring and Evaluation, Environmental Management Services, Waste Management, Environmental Management Plan, Review of Environmental Reports and Conflict Resolution and Occupational Health and Safety Consultancy

Land Surveying and GIS

Coyesa Company Limited offers Land Surveying Services such as, Cadastral, Engineering, Topographic, Surveying, Grid Surveying, Deformation Surveying, and Supervision Surveying in relating to Buildings, Road Constructions, Tunnels, Harbours and other related Projects. In GIS technology we offer a radically different way in which we produce and use the Maps required to manage our communities and industries. Hence the intelligent super Maps which are Sophisticated Planning and Analysis can be performed at the touch of a button.
